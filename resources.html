I found some sources that track trending stocks and market sentiment. Here they are:

Ape Wisdom: This is a stock sentiment tracker that monitors the most popular stock and crypto boards on Reddit and 4Chan, including WallStreetBets, r/stocks, and r/investing.

Yahoo Finance - Trending Tickers: This page shows the list of trending stocks today, including share price change and percentage, trading volume, intraday highs and lows, and day charts.

Investing.com - Trending Stocks: Provides an algorithmic analysis of stock market trends and offers popularity-based sector data, quotes, and news for a top selection of buzz-generating stocks.

Ahead of Wall Street - Daily Stock Market Outlook: This source provides an advanced look at market trends, including GDP and other economic indicators.

Yahoo Finance - Latest Stock Market News: Provides the latest news affecting the stock market, including company-specific news and broader market trends.
